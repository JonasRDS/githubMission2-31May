about me

Hello, welcome to my webpage.
I am Jonas, born and raised in Joinville, Brazil, now calling New Zealand home. I am passionate about music, having worked in the industry for over 20 years as a vocalist and guitarist. Music helps me connect to life and I love to connect with people through music.

my hometown

Joinville is the largest city in Santa Catarina, in the Southern Region of Brazil. It is mainly of Portuguese, German, Swiss, Norwegian and Italian descent. The city has a strong arts culture and it is here where my love of music began. Some of the most popular music genres are rock, sertanejo and MPB (musica popular brazileira, "brazilian popular music"). I grew up loving rock music.
